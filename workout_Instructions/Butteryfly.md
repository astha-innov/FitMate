Here are the steps to perform the Butterfly:

1) Adjust the Machine: Set the seat so handles are at chest level. Adjust arm pads so your arms are slightly behind your torso to begin.

2) Sit Tall: Place your feet flat, back pressed against the pad, and grasp the handles or rest forearms against pads (depending on machine type).

3) Set Arm Position: Keep a slight bend in the elbows to protect your joints.

4) Initiate the Movement: Exhale and bring the arms together in front of your chest in a hugging motion. Focus on squeezing your chest muscles at the peak.

5) Return With Control: Slowly reverse the movement until you feel a mild stretch in the chest. Avoid letting weights slam or overstretching.
