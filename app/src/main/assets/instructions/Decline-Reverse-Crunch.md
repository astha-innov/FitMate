# Tutorial for Decline Bent Leg Reverse Crunch

1. Keep your knees bent at a 90-degree angle and your feet together, this is your starting position.
2. Engage your core and lift your hips off the bench, pulling your knees towards your chest in a smooth and controlled motion.
3. Pause at the top of the movement for a second, then slowly lower your hips back down to the starting position.
4. Repeat this movement for your desired number of reps, ensuring to maintain control and stability throughout the exercise.
