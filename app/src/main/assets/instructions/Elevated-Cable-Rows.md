# Elevated Cable Row Instructions:

1. Sit forward on the seat or bench to grab the v-bar and place your feet on the foot support.
2. Then push your hips back, but keep your knees slightly bent.
3. Next, Pull the v-bar to your sternum while pulling with your shoulders and elbows back.
4. Return by allowing the cable to extend your arms, pull your shoulders forward and stretch forward your lower back.
5. Repeat the v-bar rows for 6-12 repetitions.
