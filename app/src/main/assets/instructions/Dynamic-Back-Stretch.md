# Dynamic Back Stretch Instructions

1. Stand tall with feet hip-width apart.
2. Bring both arms behind your back. Clasp your hands together with fingers interlaced.
3. Pull your shoulders back and down. Lift your chest tall.
4. Straighten your arms and slowly lift your clasped hands away from your body, behind you.
5. Continue lifting until you feel a deep stretch across your front shoulders and chest.
6. Keep your chest lifted — do not hunch forward.
7. Hold for 20 to 30 seconds. Breathe deeply.
8. Release slowly. Repeat two to three times.
