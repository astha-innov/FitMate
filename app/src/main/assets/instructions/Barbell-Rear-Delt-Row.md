# Barbell Rear Delt Row Instructions

1. Step up to your bar. While bending slightly at your knees and hips, grab the bar with an overhand grip.
2. Grasp the bar with your hands wider than shoulder-width, and keep your back flex and not hunched.
3. Pull the bar up to your chest with you deltoids and upper back.
4. Then slowly lower the bar straight back down by extending your arms.
5. Repeat for a nominal set of 8-12 reps.
