# Hack Squat Instructions:

1. Stand facing a sled, with feet hip-width apart and toes pointed slightly outward.
2. Place your hands on the handles of the sled.
3. Slowly squat down, pushing your hips back and keeping your chest up.
4. Drive through your heels to extend your knees and hips, and press the sled away from you.
5. Lower the sled under control and repeat.
