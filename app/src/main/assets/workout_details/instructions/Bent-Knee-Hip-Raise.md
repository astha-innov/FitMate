# Instructions for Bent Knee Hip Raise:

1. Lie flat on your back, relaxing your upper back and shoulders, and with your spine in a neutral position.
2. Bend your knees, while keeping your feet flat on the floor at all times.
3. Engage your core. Lift both legs off the floor, keeping them aligned and hold neutral spine alignment. 
4. Hold this position for a few seconds, then slowly lower your legs to the start position without letting your lower back lift.
