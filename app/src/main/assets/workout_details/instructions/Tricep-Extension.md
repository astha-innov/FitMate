# Tricep Extension Instructions

1. Begin by getting a dumbbell and lying down on a flat bench with your head at the end of the bench.
2. You will start in a position with both palms under one side of the dumbbell with the handle going down between your hands.
3. Then, lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending at your elbows.
4. Finish the movement by pulling the your arms back to straight up with your triceps.
5. Continue to do it again to complete your desirable total reps.
