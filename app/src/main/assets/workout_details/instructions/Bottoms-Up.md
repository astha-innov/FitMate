# A Step-by-Step Tutorial Bottoms-Up:

1. Slowly begin to lift the kettlebell towards your shoulder, keeping your elbow close to your body and the kettlebell bottom-up throughout the movement.
2. Once the kettlebell reaches shoulder height, pause for a moment, maintaining control and balance of the kettlebell.
3. Then, slowly lower the kettlebell back down to the starting position, ensuring the bottom of the kettlebell remains facing upwards.
4. Repeat this motion for the desired number of repetitions, then switch hands and perform the same movements with the other arm.
