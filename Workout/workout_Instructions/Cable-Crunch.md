# Cable Crunch Instructions:

1. Set the cable machine to a high position and attach a rope or handle to the cable.
2. Stand with your back to the machine with your feet shoulder-width apart.
3. Grab the rope or handle with both hands, placing your hands near your ears or temples.
4. Take a step or two away from the machine to create tension in the cable.
5. Position yourself so that your elbows are pointing out to the sides and your upper body is upright.
6. Initiate the movement by bending at the waist and bringing your elbows and head downwards, towards your knees.
7. As you crunch down, focus on contracting your abdominal muscles and exhaling.
8. Hold the crunch position briefly, squeezing your abs.
9. Slowly return to the starting position by extending your torso back up, allowing your arms to fully extend.
10. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
