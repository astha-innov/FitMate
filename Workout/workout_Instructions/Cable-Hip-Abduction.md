# Hip Abduction Instructions:

1. Start by attaching a cable cuff to your ankle from a low pulley.
2. Stand in front of the low pulley with your hip of the leg with the ankle cuff further away from the pulley.
3. If possible, grab a bar on the cable machine for support.
4. Pull the cable by raising your leg away from the pulley, keeping your leg straight, and raising at the hip.
5. Return your leg allowing the cable to pull your active leg in front of your standing leg.
6. Continue with the hip abductors for a complete set.
